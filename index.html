About MP BioReg Consulting

Who We Are

At MP BioReg Consulting, we specialize in guiding BioPharmaceutical and Biotechnology industries through the complex landscape of regulatory affairs, with a specific focus on Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Control (CMC) for biological products. Our mission is to help companies meet and exceed their CMC challenges, ensuring compliance and optimizing processes from development to market approval. "Navigating scientific and regulatory challenges in CMC? MP BioReg Consulting offers expert solutions to guide you through."

Why Choose us

Regulatory strategy optimization

Scientific and CMC regulatory advice to optimize strategies throughout product lifecycle stages.

CMC dossier authoring

Authoring and review of comprehensive CMC dossiers, from clinical trials to new marketing applications and post-approval variations, as well as authorities briefing documents

Interim management support

Management support, including interim management and collaboration with technical teams, focusing on issue and risk management

Regulatory compliance training

Training and coaching for staff and managers to enhance regulatory compliance and efficiency.

Biological product strategy

Advisory consultation to pharma and biotech companies on various aspects of strategic pathways for biological products.
